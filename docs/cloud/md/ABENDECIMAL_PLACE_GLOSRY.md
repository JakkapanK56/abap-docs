Also referred to as a fractional digit. Decimal places contain the fractional part of a decimal number and are noted to the right of the decimal separator, regardless of the number system. In the case of [decimal numbers](ABENDECIMAL_NUMBER_GLOSRY.html), the decimal places are the [places](ABENPLACE_GLOSRY.html) that come after the [decimal separator](ABENDECIMAL_POINT_SEPARAT_GLOSRY.html).
