[Regular expression](ABENREGULAR_EXPRESSION_GLOSRY.html) in accordance with [POSIX](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/POSIX) standard 1003.2. ABAP supports [POSIX syntax](ABENREGEX_POSIX_SYNTAX.html) for regular expressions by implementing the [Boost.Regex Library](http://www.boost.org/doc/libs/1_31_0/libs/regex/doc/index.html) in the [ABAP kernel](ABENKERNEL_GLOSRY.html). Regular expressions in POSIX syntax are obsolete in ABAP and should be migrated to [PCRE regular expressions](ABENPCRE_REGEX_GLOSRY.html), [XPath regular expressions](ABENXPATH_REGEX_GLOSRY.html) or [XSD regular expressions](ABENXSD_REGEX_GLOSRY.html).
