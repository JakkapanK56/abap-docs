[Database connection](ABENDATABASE_CONNECTION_GLOSRY.html) between an [AS ABAP](ABENAS_ABAP_GLOSRY.html) and its [standard database](ABENSTANDARD_DB_GLOSRY.html). A standard connection is always opened for each [work process](ABENWORK_PROCESS_GLOSRY.html) of an AS ABAP. The [ABAP database schema](ABENDATABASE_USER_GLOSRY.html) is assigned to the [database user](ABENABAP_DB_SCHEMA_GLOSRY.html) of the standard connection.
