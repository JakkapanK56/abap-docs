Two different [data types](ABENDATA_TYPE_GLOSRY.html) are convertible if a [conversion rule](ABENCONVERSION_RULE_GLOSRY.html) applies to them. Two [data objects](ABENDATA_OBJECT_GLOSRY.html) are convertible if their data types are convertible and their content allows the corresponding conversion. Data objects of [compatible](ABENCOMPATIBLE_GLOSRY.html) data types are assigned to each other without a conversion.
