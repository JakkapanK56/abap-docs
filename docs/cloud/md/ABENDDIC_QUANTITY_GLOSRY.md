Component of a [DDIC structure](ABENDDIC_STRUCTURE_GLOSRY.html), or [DDIC database table](ABENDDIC_DB_TABLE_GLOSRY.html) of type `QUAN` defined in the [ABAP Dictionary](ABENABAP_DICTIONARY_GLOSRY.html). A quantity field must be linked to a [unit key](ABENUNIT_GLOSRY.html) of a structure, database table, or view. This key determines the unit of the quantity field.
