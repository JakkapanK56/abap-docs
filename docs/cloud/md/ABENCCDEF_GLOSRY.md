[Include program](ABENINCLUDE_PROGRAM_GLOSRY.html) of a [class pool](ABENCLASS_POOL_GLOSRY.html) that is included in front of the [declaration part](ABENDECLARATION_PART_GLOSRY.html) of the [global class](ABENGLOBAL_CLASS_GLOSRY.html). It can be edited in [ADT](ABENADT_GLOSRY.html) by navigating to *Class-Relevant Local Types*. The internal name of the include program ends with `CCDEF`. See also [CCIMP include](ABENCCIMP_GLOSRY.html).
