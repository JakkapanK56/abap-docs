Operand whose content is passed to or from a [formal parameter](ABENFORMAL_PARAMETER_GLOSRY.html) when a callable unit, such as a [procedure](ABENPROCEDURE_GLOSRY.html) or [function](ABENFUNCTION_GLOSRY.html), is called. An actual parameter passed to a [built-in function](ABENBUILTIN_FUNCTION_GLOSRY.html) is also called [argument](ABENARGUMENT_GLOSRY.html) of the function.
