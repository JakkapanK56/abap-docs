Most [data types](ABENDATA_TYPE_GLOSRY.html) are subject to specific alignment requirements depending on the current platform. In memory, [fields](ABENFIELD_GLOSRY.html) of this [type](ABENTYPE_GLOSRY.html) must start at addresses that are divisible by 2, 4, 8, or 16. In a [structure](ABENSTRUCTURE_GLOSRY.html) or [substructure](ABENSUBSTRUCTURE_GLOSRY.html), the [structure component](ABENSTRUCTURE_COMPONENT_GLOSRY.html) with the highest alignment requirement determines the alignment of the entire structure, where [alignment gaps](ABENALIGNMENT_GAP_GLOSRY.html) are created before or after components with alignment requirements.
