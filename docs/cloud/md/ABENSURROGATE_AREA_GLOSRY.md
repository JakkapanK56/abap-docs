Subset of the characters that can be represented in [Unicode](ABENUNICODE_GLOSRY.html) (`U+10000` to `U+10FFFF`) that includes, for example, musical notes or special Asian characters. Addressed directly in [UTF-8](ABENUTF8_GLOSRY.html) and [UTF-32](ABENUTF32_GLOSRY.html); addressed using [surrogates](ABENSURROGATES_GLOSRY.html) in [UTF-16](ABENUTF16_GLOSRY.html). [UCS-2](ABENUCS2_GLOSRY.html) (supported by the ABAP programming language) does not support characters from the surrogate area. ABAP interprets a character from the surrogate area as two characters.
