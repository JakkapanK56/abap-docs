An [expression](ABENEXP_GLOSRY.html) in an [ABAP statement](ABENABAP_STATEMENT_GLOSRY.html). Expressions can be, for example, calls of [built-in functions](ABENBUILTIN_FUNCTION_GLOSRY.html) or [functional method calls](ABENFUNCTIONAL_METHOD_CALL_GLOSRY.html), [declaration expressions](ABENDECLARATION_POSITION_GLOSRY.html), [logical expressions](ABENLOGICAL_EXPRESSION_GLOSRY.html) constructed from [relational expressions](ABENRELATIONAL_EXPRESSION_GLOSRY.html), [constructor expressions](ABENCONSTRUCTOR_EXPRESSION_GLOSRY.html), [table expressions](ABENTABLE_EXPRESSION_GLOSRY.html), and [calculation expressions](ABENCALCULATION_EXPRESSION_GLOSRY.html). See also [SQL Expression](ABENSQL_EXPRESSION_GLOSRY.html).
