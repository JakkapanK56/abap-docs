Locks control the access to data by multiple users. Regarding access to [database tables](ABENDATABASE_TABLE_GLOSRY.html), there are [shared locks](ABENSHARED_LOCK_GLOSRY.html) or [exclusive locks](ABENEXCLUSIVE_LOCK_GLOSRY.html) implemented as [database locks](ABENDATABASE_LOCK_GLOSRY.html) and [SAP locks](ABENSAP_LOCK_GLOSRY.html). Regarding access to [shared objects](ABENSHARED_OBJECTS_GLOSRY.html), there are [area locks](ABENAREA_LOCK_GLOSRY.html).
