Special [instance method](ABENINSTANCE_METHOD_GLOSRY.html) of a [test class](ABENTEST_CLASS_GLOSRY.html) in which a test is implemented. Test methods are defined using the addition [`FOR TESTING`](ABAPMETHODS_TESTING.html) of the statement [`METHODS`](ABAPMETHODS.html), and called during a [test run](ABENTEST_RUN_GLOSRY.html) of the [ABAP Unit](ABENABAP_UNIT_GLOSRY.html) tool.
