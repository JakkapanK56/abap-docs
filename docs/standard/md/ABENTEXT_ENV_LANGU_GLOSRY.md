Language of the [text environment](ABENTEXT_ENVIRONMENT_GLOSRY.html) of the current [internal session](ABENINTERNAL_SESSION_GLOSRY.html). The text environment language is the content of the [system field](ABENSYSTEM_FIELD_GLOSRY.html)\\ [`sy-langu`](ABENSYSTEM_FIELDS.html). At the beginning of each internal session, it is the [logon language](ABENLOGON_LANGUAGE_GLOSRY.html) and can be set explicitly with [`SET LOCALE LANGUAGE`](ABAPSET_LOCALE.html).
