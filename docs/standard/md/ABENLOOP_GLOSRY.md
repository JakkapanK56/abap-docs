[Control structure](ABENCONTROL_STRUCTURE_GLOSRY.html) that performs an [iteration](ABENITERATION_GLOSRY.html). Consists of a [statement block](ABENSTATEMENT_BLOCK_GLOSRY.html) defined by the keywords [`DO` - `ENDDO`](ABAPDO.html), [`WHILE` - `ENDWHILE`](ABAPWHILE.html), [`LOOP` - `ENDLOOP`](ABAPLOOP_AT_ITAB.html), [`PROVIDE`  - `ENDPROVIDE`](ABAPPROVIDE.html), [`SELECT` - `ENDSELECT`](ABAPSELECT.html) and that can be executed multiple times.
