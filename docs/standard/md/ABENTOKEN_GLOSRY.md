Elementary component of an [ABAP statement](ABENABAP_STATEMENT_GLOSRY.html), into which the compiler breaks down statements into tokens. Tokens are [ABAP words](ABENABAP_WORD_GLOSRY.html), [ABAP operands](ABENABAP_OPERAND_GLOSRY.html), [operators](ABENOPERATOR_GLOSRY.html), and certain special characters.
