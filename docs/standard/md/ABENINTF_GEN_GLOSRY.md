Boundary between two [contexts](ABENOBJ_CONTEXT_GLOSRY.html).

-   [Procedures](ABENPROCEDURE_GLOSRY.html) have a [parameter interface](ABENPARAMETER_INTERFACE_GLOSRY.html).
-   In [classes](ABENCLASS_GLOSRY.html), interfaces are defined by [class components](ABENCLASS_COMPONENT_GLOSRY.html) of the different [visibility sections](ABENVISIBILITY_SECTION_GLOSRY.html).
-   The [public](ABENPUBLIC_GLOSRY.html) components of classes can also be defined in standalone [interfaces](ABENOO_INTF_GLOSRY.html) of [ABAP Objects](ABENABAP_OBJECTS_GLOSRY.html).
-   The [database interface](ABENDATABASE_INTERFACE_GLOSRY.html) connects an [AS ABAP](ABENAS_ABAP_GLOSRY.html) to its [database](ABENDATABASE_GLOSRY.html).
-   In special cases, [interface work areas](ABENINTERFACE_WORK_AREA_GLOSRY.html) are used between programs of a [program group](ABENPROGRAM_GROUP_GLOSRY.html).
-   The global data of the ABAP program or the interface work areas can be used as an interface between [ABAP programs](ABENABAP_PROGRAM_GLOSRY.html) and [dynpros](ABENDYNPRO_GLOSRY.html).
