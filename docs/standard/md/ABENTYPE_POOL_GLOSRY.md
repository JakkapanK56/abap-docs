[ABAP program](ABENABAP_PROGRAM_GLOSRY.html), administrated by the [ABAP Dictionary](ABENABAP_DICTIONARY_GLOSRY.html), that is introduced by the statement [`TYPE-POOL`](ABAPTYPE-POOL.html) and which contains the definitions of globally visible [data types](ABENDATA_TYPE_GLOSRY.html), [constants](ABENCONSTANT_GLOSRY.html), and [macros](ABENMACRO_GLOSRY.html). A type pool is loaded the first time its data types and constants are used. It does not support own [dynpros](ABENDYNPRO_GLOSRY.html).
