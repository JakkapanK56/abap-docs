Calculates a value. Either an [arithmetic expression](ABENARITHMETIC_EXPRESSION_GLOSRY.html), a [bit expression](ABENBIT_EXPRESSION_GLOSRY.html), or a [string expression](ABENSTRING_EXPRESSION_GLOSRY.html). The three calculation expression types can be used simultaneously as operands of [relational expressions](ABENRELATIONAL_EXPRESSION_GLOSRY.html), but they cannot be combined within a calculation expression.
