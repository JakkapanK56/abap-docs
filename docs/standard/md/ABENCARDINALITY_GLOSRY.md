In a [join](ABENJOIN_GLOSRY.html) of two [SQL data sources](ABENSQL_DATA_SOURCE_GLOSRY.html), the cardinality expresses the relation between the rows of the data sources. Typical cardinalities are one-to-one (`1:1`), one-to-many (`1:n`), many-to-one (`n:1`), and many-to-many (`n:m`).
