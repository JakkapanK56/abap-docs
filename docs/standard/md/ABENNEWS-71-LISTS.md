The new addition [`ENVIRONMENT TIME FORMAT`](ABAPWRITE_TO_OPTIONS.html) to the `WRITE TO` and `WRITE` statements supports the new [12 hour format](ABENNEWS-710-OTHERS.html).

The [predefined time formats](ABENWRITE_FORMATS.html) of the statements `WRITE TO` and `WRITE` cannot be affected automatically due to downward compatibility.

This modification was also added to ABAP release 7.0 in SP14.
