[SAP HANA XSA](ABENXSA_GLOSRY.html) replaces [SAP HANA XSC](ABENXSC_GLOSRY.html). The [access methods](ABENSAP_HANA_ACCESS_XSC.html) designed specifically for XSC objects are not suitable for XSA objects and have been declared obsolete. [DDIC external views](ABENDDIC_EXTERNAL_VIEWS.html) are one of those access methods. They have been declared obsolete. More information can be found in SAP Notes [2465027](https://launchpad.support.sap.com/#/notes/2465027) and [3116165](https://launchpad.support.sap.com/#/notes/3116165).
