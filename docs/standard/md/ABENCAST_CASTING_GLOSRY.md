Handling of a [data object](ABENDATA_OBJECT_GLOSRY.html) by assuming a specific [data type](ABENDATA_TYPE_GLOSRY.html). Explicit casting is possible in the statement [`ASSIGN`](ABAPASSIGN.html) with the addition [`ASSIGNING`](ABAPREAD_TABLE_OUTDESC.html) when processing internal tables, for some assignments of [actual parameters](ABENACTUAL_PARAMETER_GLOSRY.html) to [formal parameters](ABENFORMAL_PARAMETER_GLOSRY.html), and as a [downcast](ABENDOWN_CAST_GLOSRY.html) or [upcast](ABENUP_CAST_GLOSRY.html) in the [assignments](ABENASSIGNMENT_GLOSRY.html) between [reference variables](ABENREFERENCE_VARIABLE_GLOSRY.html). An implicit casting sometimes takes place when handling [operands](ABENOPERAND_GLOSRY.html) in certain [operand positions](ABENOPERAND_POSITION_GLOSRY.html).
