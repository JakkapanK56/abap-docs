Number of significant [places](ABENFRACT_DIGIT_GLOSRY.html) in a [decimal floating point number](ABENDECFLOAT_GLOSRY.html). These are the places of the mantissa after the leading zeros. The precision is at least one and at most the length of the mantissa (16 or 34). See also [scaling](ABENSCALE_GLOSRY.html).
