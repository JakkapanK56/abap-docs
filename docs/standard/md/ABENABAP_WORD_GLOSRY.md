Word of the ABAP programming language. ABAP words are the [tokens](ABENTOKEN_GLOSRY.html) of an [ABAP statement](ABENABAP_STATEMENT_GLOSRY.html) and express its semantics. In ABAP statements, ABAP words are either used as [ABAP keywords](ABENABAP_KEYWORD_GLOSRY.html) or as additions. A single ABAP word can be both a keyword and an addition. Combinations of ABAP words form [ABAP language elements](ABENABAP_LANGUAGE_ELEMENT_GLOSRY.html) or [ABAP language element additions](ABENABAP_LANGUAGE_ELMNT_ADD_GLOSRY.html).
