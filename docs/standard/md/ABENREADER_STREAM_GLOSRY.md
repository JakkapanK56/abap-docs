Also referred to as [input stream](ABENINPUT_STREAM_GLOSRY.html). Either a [data stream](ABENDATA_STREAM_GLOSRY.html) that is connected to a [streaming data source](ABENSTREAMING_DATA_SOURCE_GLOSRY.html) or a [filter stream](ABENFILTER_STREAM_GLOSRY.html) that reads data from another stream. The direction of a reader stream is always away from the data source. Data can only be read from a reader stream.
