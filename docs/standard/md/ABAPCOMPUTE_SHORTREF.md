`COMPUTE [EXACT] lhs =|?= rhs.`

**Obsolete:** Assigns the value of a right side `rhs` to a left side `lhs`.

-   `EXACT`\\
    For certain arithmetic expressions on the right side, forces a [lossless calculation](ABENLOSSLESS_CALCULATION_GLOSRY.html) without rounding.
