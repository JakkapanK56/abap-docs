8-bit [Unicode character representation](ABENUNICODE_CHAR_REPRESENT_GLOSRY.html). [ASCII](ABENASCII_GLOSRY.html) characters are represented by a single byte. Other European characters are represented by two bytes. Most Asian characters are represented by three bytes. The characters in the [surrogate area](ABENSURROGATE_AREA_GLOSRY.html) are addressed directly using four bytes.
