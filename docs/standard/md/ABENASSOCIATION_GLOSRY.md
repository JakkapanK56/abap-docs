Relationship between [association sources](ABENASSOCIATION_SOURCE_GLOSRY.html) and [association targets](ABENASSOCIATION_TARGET_GLOSRY.html) defined by conditions.

The following types of associations are available:

An association can be [source-resolved](ABENSOURCE_RESOLVED_ASSOC_GLOSRY.html) or [target-resolved](ABENTARGET_RESOLVED_ASSOC_GLOSRY.html), depending on the location of the foreign key field.

-   [CTE associations](ABENCTE_ASSOCIATION_GLOSRY.html)
