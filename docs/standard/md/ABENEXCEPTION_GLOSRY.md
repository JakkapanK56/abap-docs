Error situation during execution of an [ABAP program](ABENABAP_PROGRAM_GLOSRY.html). Exceptions are [catchable](ABENCATCHABLE_EXCEPTION_GLOSRY.html) (statements [`TRY`](ABAPTRY.html), [`CATCH`](ABAPCATCH_TRY.html)) or [uncatchable](ABENUNCATCHABLE_EXCEPTION_GLOSRY.html). Uncaught exceptions result in a [runtime error](ABENRUNTIME_ERROR_GLOSRY.html).
