`LOOP AT SCREEN INTO wa.    ...  ENDLOOP.`

Reads the properties of the screen elements of the current [dynpro](ABENDYNPRO_GLOSRY.html) in a loop and assigns the results to a work area `wa` of the structure [`SCREEN`](ABENSCREEN.html).
