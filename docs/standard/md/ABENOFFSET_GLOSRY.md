Specified position for a character or a byte in a [character-like](ABENCHARLIKE_DATA_OBJECT_GLOSRY.html) or [byte-like data object](ABENBYTE_LIKE_DATA_OBJECT_GLOSRY.html) where counting begins with 0. The value of an offset can also be interpreted as the identifier for the beginning of the line, the spacing between the characters or bytes, and the end of the line.
