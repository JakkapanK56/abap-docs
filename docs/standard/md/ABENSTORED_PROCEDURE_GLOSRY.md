[Database procedure](ABENDATABASE_PROCEDURE_GLOSRY.html). Procedural [database object](ABENDB_OBJECT_GLOSRY.html) that can be implemented and called depending on the platform. A stored procedure generally combines sequences of multiple [SQL](ABENSQL_GLOSRY.html) statements and contains additional instructions for controlling the sequence. [ABAP-managed](ABENABAP_MANAGED_DB_OBJECT_GLOSRY.html) stored procedures are [AMDP procedures](ABENAMDP_PROCEDURE_GLOSRY.html) and [AMDP functions](ABENAMDP_FUNCTION_GLOSRY.html).
