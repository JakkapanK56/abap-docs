Bytes within a [structure](ABENSTRUCTURE_GLOSRY.html) that are inserted either before [structure components](ABENSTRUCTURE_COMPONENT_GLOSRY.html) with alignment requirements or before the end of the structure to provide the necessary [alignment](ABENALIGNMENT_GLOSRY.html). Alignment gaps contribute to the length of a structure. The binary content of alignment gaps is not defined and must not be evaluated.
