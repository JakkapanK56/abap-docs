In [ABAP CDS](ABENABAP_CDS_GLOSRY.html), a special handling for amounts and quantities is implemented. [ABAP CDS amount fields](ABENCDS_AMOUNT_FIELD_GLOSRY.html) and [ABAP CDS quantity fields](ABENCDS_QUANTITY_GLOSRY.html) are similar to [DDIC currency fields](ABENDDIC_CURRENCY_FIELD_GLOSRY.html) and [DDIC quantity fields](ABENDDIC_QUANTITY_GLOSRY.html), but more data types are possible.

In [CDS view entities](ABENCDS_V2_VIEW_GLOSRY.html), the [currency key](ABENCURRENCY_KEY_GLOSRY.html) and [unit key](ABENUNIT_GLOSRY.html) reference is respected in [expressions](ABENCDS_EXPRESSIONS_V2.html), [comparisons](ABENCDS_COND_EXPR_TYPES_V2.html), [`UNION` views](ABENCDS_UNION_V2.html), and [`INTERSECT` views](ABENCDS_INTERSECT_V2.html). Moreover, a special data type is available: the [calculated quantity with calculated unit reference](ABENCDS_CALCULATED_QUANTITY.html).

Read the following sections for details:

-   [ABAP CDS Amount Fields](ABENCDS_AMOUNT_FIELD.html)
-   [ABAP CDS Quantity Fields](ABENCDS_QUANTITY_FIELD.html)
-   [ABAP CDS Calculated Quantity with Calculated Unit](ABENCDS_CALCULATED_QUANTITY.html)
