Contexts are obsolete [repository objects](ABENREPOSITORY_OBJECT_GLOSRY.html) that are composed of [key fields](ABENKEY_FIELD_GLOSRY.html) to be entered, relationships between these fields, and further fields that can be derived from them. They are defined [abstractly](ABENABSTRACT_GLOSRY.html) in the [Context Builder](ABENCONTEXT_BUILDER_GLOSRY.html) in the [ABAP Workbench](ABENABAP_WORKBENCH_GLOSRY.html) and used during runtime as temporary objects. Contexts can be processed with the statements [`CONTEXTS`](ABAPCONTEXTS.html), [`DEMAND`](ABAPDEMAND.html), and [`SUPPLY`](ABAPSUPPLY.html).
