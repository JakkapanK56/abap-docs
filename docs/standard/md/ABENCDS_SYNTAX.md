The syntax of the [CDS DDL](ABENCDS_DDL_GLOSRY.html) and the [CDS DCL](ABENCDS_DCL_GLOSRY.html) in ABAP CDS comprises elements of the general [SQL](ABENSQL_GLOSRY.html)\\ [DDL](ABENDDL_GLOSRY.html) and [DCL](ABENDCL_GLOSRY.html) and also makes it possible to define annotations and CDS associations. The syntax and semantics basically match the general [CDS](ABENCDS_GLOSRY.html) concept.
