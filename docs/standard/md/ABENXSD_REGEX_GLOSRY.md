[Regular expressions](ABENREGULAR_EXPRESSION_GLOSRY.html) using the syntax and semantics described under [Regular expressions in XSD 1.0 and 1.1](https://www.w3.org/XML/2008/03/xsdl-regex/re.xml). ABAP supports [XSD syntax](ABENREGEX_XSD_SYNTAX.html) for regular expressions by transforming them to [PCRE](ABENPCRE_GLOSRY.html) internally. See also [PCRE regular expression](ABENPCRE_REGEX_GLOSRY.html), [XPath regular expression](ABENXPATH_REGEX_GLOSRY.html) and [POSIX regular expression](ABENPOSIX_REGEX_GLOSRY.html) (obsolete).
