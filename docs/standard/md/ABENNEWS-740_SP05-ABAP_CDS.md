The new [ABAP CDS](ABENCDS.html) is the ABAP-specific implementation of the general [Core Data Services (CDS)](ABENCORE_DATA_SERVICES_GLOSRY.html). In its first phase, ABAP CDS provides a [DDL](ABENCDS_F1_DDL_SYNTAX.html) for the definition of

[CDS views](ABENCDS_V1_VIEWS.html), used together with ABAP SQL to perform reads.
