[Checkpoint](ABENCHECKPOINT_GLOSRY.html) that is assigned to a [checkpoint group](ABENCHECKPOINT_GROUP_GLOSRY.html) using the addition `ID` of the statements [`ASSERT`](ABAPASSERT.html), [`BREAK-POINT`](ABAPBREAK-POINT.html), or [`LOG-POINT`](ABAPLOG-POINT.html). The behavior of an activatable checkpoint is controlled by either group-specific or program-specific activation settings, where program-specific settings apply to all activatable checkpoints of a program and overwrite the settings of the relevant checkpoint group.
