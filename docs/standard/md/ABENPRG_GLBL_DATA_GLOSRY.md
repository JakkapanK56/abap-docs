Global data for short. [Data object](ABENDATA_OBJECT_GLOSRY.html) that is declared in an [ABAP program](ABENABAP_PROGRAM_GLOSRY.html) and is known in the entire program behind its declaration. Global data is declared in the [global declaration part](ABENGLOBAL_DECLARATION_SECT_GLOSRY.html) of a program. See also [local data](ABENLOCAL_DATA_GLOSRY.html).
