`CLASS-DATA attr options.`

Declares a [static attribute](ABENSTATIC_ATTRIBUTE_GLOSRY.html)\\ `attr` of a class or an interface.

-   `options`\\
    As in [`DATA`](ABAPDATA_SHORTREF.html), except that the addition `WITH HEADER LINE` cannot be specified.
