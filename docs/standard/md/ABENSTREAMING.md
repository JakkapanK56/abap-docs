Streaming describes how [streams](ABENSTREAM_GLOSRY.html) are passed. Streams are represented by instances of special classes. The transfer of streams takes place by calling the relevant methods of the object.

Streams are classified by the following properties:

-   Stream kind
-   [Data streams](ABENDATA_STREAM_GLOSRY.html) and [filter streams](ABENFILTER_STREAM_GLOSRY.html)
-   Stream direction
-   [Reader streams](ABENREADER_STREAM_GLOSRY.html) and [writer streams](ABENWRITER_STREAM_GLOSRY.html)
-   Stream type
-   [Binary streams](ABENBINARY_STREAM_GLOSRY.html) and [character streams](ABENCHARACTER_STREAM_GLOSRY.html)

-   [Classes and interfaces for streams](ABENSTREAMING_CLASSES_INTERFACES.html)
-   [Streaming for Data Objects](ABENDATA_OBJECTS_STREAMS.html)
-   [Streaming in ABAP SQL](ABENABAP_SQL_STREAMING.html)
