Superordinate term for [comparison operators](ABENCOMP_OPERATOR_GLOSRY.html) and [predicate operators](ABENPREDICATE_OPERATOR_GLOSRY.html). Relational operators occur in [relational expressions](ABENRELATIONAL_EXPRESSION_GLOSRY.html). Relational operators belong to the [logical operators](ABENLOGICAL_OPERATOR_GLOSRY.html).
