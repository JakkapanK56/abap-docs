Also called root annotation. The [CDS annotation](ABENCDS_ANNOTATION_GLOSRY.html) defined after the statement [`DEFINE ANNOTATION`](ABENCDS_F1_DEFINE_ANNOTATION.html) in a [CDS annotation definition](ABENCDS_ANNOTATION_GLOSRY.html), or a name of an [annotation](ABENCDS_ANNOTATION_GLOSRY.html) specified on its own or in front of a period in the [annotation syntax](ABENCDS_ANNOTATION_SYNTAX_GLOSRY.html). A main annotation can be structured using [subannotations](ABENSUB_ANNOTATION_GLOSRY.html).
