A [chaining](ABENCHAINING_GLOSRY.html) in which multiple [functional methods](ABENFUNCTIONAL_METHOD_GLOSRY.html) or [constructor expressions](ABENCONSTRUCTOR_EXPRESSION_GLOSRY.html) are linked via [component selectors](ABENCOMPONENT_SELECTOR_GLOSRY.html). The [return values](ABENRETURN_VALUE_GLOSRY.html) of the individual methods are references to the next methods. A chained method call can either be executed as a [functional method call](ABAPCALL_METHOD_FUNCTIONAL.html) or as a [standalone statement](ABAPCALL_METHOD_STATIC_SHORT.html).
