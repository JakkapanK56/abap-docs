[Reference](ABAPMOVE_OBS.html)

`MOVE \{[EXACT] source  TO destination\}\     |\ \{        source ?TO destination\}.`

**Obsolete:** Assigns the content of `source` to `destination`. To assign a general reference variable to a more specialized one, `?TO` must be used.

-   `EXACT`\\
    Forces a [lossless assignment](ABENLOSSLESS_ASSIGNMENT_GLOSRY.html).
