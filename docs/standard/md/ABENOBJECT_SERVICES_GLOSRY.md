OS for short. [Interfaces](ABENOO_INTF_GLOSRY.html) and [classes](ABENCLASS_GLOSRY.html) from the [class library](ABENCLASS_LIBRARY_GLOSRY.html) with the prefixes `IF_OS_` and `CL_OS_`. Object services provide [persistence services](ABENPERSISTENCE_SERVICE_GLOSRY.html) and [transaction services](ABENTRANSACTION_SERVICE_GLOSRY.html) that are used to manage [persistent objects](ABENPERSISTENT_OBJECT_GLOSRY.html) and object-oriented transactions. There is also a [query service](ABENQUERY_SERVICE_GLOSRY.html) for finding and loading persistent objects.
