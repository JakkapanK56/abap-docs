`EXIT.`

Exits a [loop](ABAPEXIT_LOOP.html) or a [processing block](ABAPEXIT_PROCESSING_BLOCKS.html). Within a loop, the entire loop processing is terminated and the program resumes after the loop. Outside of a loop, the current processing block is terminated.
