Short for Runtime exit. Rabax is an internal SAP term describing a program crash after a [runtime error](ABENRUNTIME_ERROR_GLOSRY.html). The corresponding [ABAP kernel](ABENKERNEL_GLOSRY.html) module, also called Rabax, performs the tasks required to complete the [internal session](ABENINTERNAL_SESSION_GLOSRY.html) and creates the associated [short dump](ABENSHORT_DUMP_GLOSRY.html).
