The source and result of a general XSL transformation are [XML](ABENXML_GLOSRY.html) data. When an XSL transformation is called using the statement `CALL TRANSFORMATION`, however, ABAP data can also be transformed directly to XML and vice versa. For this purpose, a [serialization](ABENSERIALIZATION_GLOSRY.html) or [deserialization](ABENDESERIALIZATION_GLOSRY.html) is performed implicitly, with [asXML](ABENASXML_GLOSRY.html) as an intermediate format.
