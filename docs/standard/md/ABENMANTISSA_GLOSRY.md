A mantissa is an uninterrupted sequence of a *+* sign or a *\-* sign and a sequence of digits that can have a maximum of one period (*.*) as a decimal separator at an arbitrary position. A mantissa is used in [scientific notations](ABENSCIENTIFIC_NOTATION_GLOSRY.html) for the representation of numbers.
