The following table provides a general overview of important operand positions in ABAP SQL and which operands can be used.

Certain operand positions are subject to further restrictions and these are documented where appropriate.

-   The arguments of [aggregate expressions](ABAPSELECT_AGGREGATE.html) can be [SQL expressions](ABAPSQL_EXPR.html) with the exception of [aggregate expressions](ABAPSELECT_AGGREGATE.html) and [window expressions](ABAPSELECT_OVER.html).
-   The arguments of [window expressions](ABAPSELECT_OVER.html) can be [SQL expressions](ABAPSQL_EXPR.html) with the exception of [window expressions](ABAPSELECT_OVER.html).
