`... \{\ [+|-] operand1        [\{+|-|*|/|DIV|MOD|**\}\ [+|-] operand2        [\{+|-|*|/|DIV|MOD|**\}\ [+|-] operand3        ... ]]\ \} ...`

Arithmetic expression for calculating a numeric result from the operand `operand`. Arithmetic expressions can be parenthesized explicitly using [`( )`](ABENARITH_BRACKETS.html) and can occur in the [read positions of certain statements](ABENOPERANDS_EXPRESSIONS.html).

-   [`+|-`](ABAPCOMPUTE_ARITH.html)\\
    Sign of an operand `operand`.
-   [`+|-|*|/|DIV|MOD|**`](ABENARITH_OPERATORS.html)\\
    Arithmetic operators.
