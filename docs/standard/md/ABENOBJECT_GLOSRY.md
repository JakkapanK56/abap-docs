[Instance](ABENINSTANCE_GLOSRY.html) of a [type](ABENTYPE_GLOSRY.html). Either an instance of a [class](ABENCLASS_GLOSRY.html) or, as a [data object](ABENDATA_OBJECT_GLOSRY.html), an instance of a [data type](ABENDATA_TYPE_GLOSRY.html). Exists in the [internal session](ABENINTERNAL_SESSION_GLOSRY.html) of an [ABAP program](ABENABAP_PROGRAM_GLOSRY.html) or as a [shared object](ABENSHARED_OBJECT_GLOSRY.html) in the [shared memory](ABENSHARED_MEMORY_GLOSRY.html). There are also [persistent objects](ABENPERSISTENT_OBJECT_GLOSRY.html) in the [database](ABENDATABASE_GLOSRY.html).
