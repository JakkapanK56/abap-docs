[Data object](ABENDATA_OBJECT_GLOSRY.html) with a [structured type](ABENSTRUCTURED_TYPE_GLOSRY.html) that consists of [structure components](ABENSTRUCTURE_COMPONENT_GLOSRY.html). In [ABAP programs](ABENABAP_PROGRAM_GLOSRY.html), whole structures or just components of structures can be accessed. Structures that contain only [character-like](ABENCHARLIKE_DATA_OBJECT_GLOSRY.html), [flat](ABENFLAT_GLOSRY.html)\\ [components](ABENSTRUCTURE_COMPONENT_GLOSRY.html) can also be handled like [elementary data objects](ABENELEMENTARY_DATA_OBJECT_GLOSRY.html).
