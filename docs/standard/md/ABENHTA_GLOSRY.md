HTA for short. Infrastructure that links the transport of database objects of the [SAP HANA database](ABENHANA_DATABASE_GLOSRY.html) ([SAP HANA Repository](ABENSAP_HANA_REPOSITORY_GLOSRY.html) objects and [HDI objects](ABENHDI_OBJECT_GLOSRY.html)) to the [Change and Transport System (CTS)](ABENCTS_GLOSRY.html) of ABAP.
