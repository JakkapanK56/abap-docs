`BACK.`

Positions the [list cursor](ABENLIST_CURSOR_GLOSRY.html) at the first position of a line block defined with `RESERVE`, a [page header](ABENPAGE_HEADER_GLOSRY.html) defined after `TOP-OF-PAGE`, or the first line underneath the page header.
