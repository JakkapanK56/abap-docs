[CDS annotation](ABENCDS_ANNOTATION_GLOSRY.html) that is specified in curly brackets [`\{ \}`](ABENCDS_F1_DEFINE_ANNOTATION.html) in an [annotation definition](ABENCDS_ANNO_DEFINITION_GLOSRY.html) after another annotation. When specifying a sub-annotation in [annotation syntax](ABENCDS_ANNOTATION_SYNTAX_GLOSRY.html), curly brackets [`\{ \}`](ABENCDS_ANNOTATIONS_SYNTAX.html) or [structured names](ABENCDS_ANNOTATIONS_SYNTAX_NAME.html) separated by periods are also used.
