`EXIT FROM STEP-LOOP.`

Exits the loop processing of a [table control](ABENTABLE_CONTROL_GLOSRY.html) or [step loop](ABENSTEP_LOOP_GLOSRY.html) in the current [dynpro](ABENDYNPRO_GLOSRY.html). Processing continues in the [dynpro flow logic](ABENDYNPRO_FLOW_LOGIC_GLOSRY.html) after the loop.
