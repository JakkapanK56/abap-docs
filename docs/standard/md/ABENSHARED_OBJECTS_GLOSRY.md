Concept of storing [objects](ABENOBJECT_GLOSRY.html) as [shared objects](ABENSHARED_OBJECT_GLOSRY.html) in the [shared memory](ABENSHARED_MEMORY_GLOSRY.html). The storage of [instances](ABENINSTANCE_GLOSRY.html) of [classes](ABENCLASS_GLOSRY.html) and of [anonymous data objects](ABENANONYMOUS_DATA_OBJECT_GLOSRY.html) is supported. Any data objects can be stored as [attributes](ABENATTRIBUTE_GLOSRY.html) of classes.
