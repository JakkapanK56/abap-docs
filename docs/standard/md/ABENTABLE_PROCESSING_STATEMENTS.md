-   Special [variants](ABENMESH_PATH_USAGE.html) of the statements shown here can access the last path node of a [mesh path](ABENMESH_PATHES.html), instead of accessing directly specified internal tables.
-   Internal tables can also be specified as a [data source](ABAPSELECT_ITAB.html) of a [query](ABENASQL_QUERY_GLOSRY.html) in ABAP SQL.
-   As well as the statements shown here, a range of [obsolete processing statements](ABENITAB_OBSOLETE.html) exists for internal tables.
