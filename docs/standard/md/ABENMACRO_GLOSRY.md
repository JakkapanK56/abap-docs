Combination of a statement sequence for internal reuse of [source code](ABENSOURCE_CODE_GLOSRY.html) within a program. In [ABAP source code](ABENABAP_SOURCE_CODE_GLOSRY.html), macros are defined between [`DEFINE`](ABAPDEFINE.html) and [`END-OF-DEFINITION`](ABAPEND-OF-DEFINITION.html) or in database table `TRMAC`.
