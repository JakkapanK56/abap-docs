ADT for short. Eclipse-based tools for editing [repository objects](ABENREPOSITORY_OBJECT_GLOSRY.html) of the [ABAP Repository](ABENABAP_REPOSITORY_GLOSRY.html) and their components. The ADT currently cover a subset of the [ABAP Workbench](ABENABAP_WORKBENCH_GLOSRY.html) functions. In the case of newer repository objects, sometimes only ADT can be used.
