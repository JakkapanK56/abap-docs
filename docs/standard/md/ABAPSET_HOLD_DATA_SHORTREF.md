[Reference](ABAPSET_HOLD_DATA.html)

`SET HOLD DATA \{ON|OFF\}.`

Enables or disables the standard menu entries *Hold Data*, *Set Data*, and *Delete Data* in the [GUI status](ABENGUI_STATUS_GLOSRY.html) of the current [dynpro](ABENDYNPRO_GLOSRY.html).
